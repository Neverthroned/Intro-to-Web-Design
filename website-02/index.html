The Queen's Gambit

The queen's gambit is one of the most popular chess openings, and one of the oldest being created in 1490. It's mostly played in lower rated games due to its easy accessibility, as well as it being a sound opening for the person playing white.

To start a queen's gambit, move the d2 pawn to d4


If black decides to follow the queen's gambit, they will move their d7 pawn to d5


The final step is for white to move their c2 pawn to c4, seemingly sacrificing it, and creating the name, queen's gambit
