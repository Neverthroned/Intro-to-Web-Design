About Shakespeare


   William Shakespeare was a renowned english poet, playwright and actor
    born in 1564. His first works were not published until 1593 and 1594, however
    he wrote 38 plays over the course of his life, many of which are still shown
    today. Shakespeare used a unique poem writing style called an
    lambic pentameter to write many of his works.
    Of all of them, Romeo and Juliet, Hamlet and Macbeth are considered
    his most famous works
