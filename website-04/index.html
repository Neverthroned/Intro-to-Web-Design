Burkes Art

Creating Individuality for the Individual

Art allows us to express ourselves; our ideas are a wonderfully complex mess that can be strung together, and that is what I do. I make a variety of things, whether it be original characters, landscapes, or cosmic horrors that should have never been introduced upon our eyes. Any art style, from renaissance look alikes, to modern... I enjoy it all. Even this website is a culmination of my web design expertise. This is essentially my mock portfolio, I had a lot of fun making it, and I had a lot of fun viewing all of my fellow MART 341 classmates' work. It was fun working with you all, and I hope you all have a great rest of your summer.
